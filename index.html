Privacy Policy

"Hajimetekiroku" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values the protection of user personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App.

1. Collection of Personal Information

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or phone number). All data entered into the App is stored solely on the user's device (or in the cloud storage configured by the user).

2. Analysis of Usage

To improve our services, the App may analyze non-personally identifiable statistical usage data (such as crash reports). This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify any individual user.

3. Compliance with Laws

The App complies with all applicable laws and regulations of Japan.
